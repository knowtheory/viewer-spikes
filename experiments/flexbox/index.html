DocumentViewer Experiments

The DocumentViewer project is a major front end component of the DocumentCloud platform. Originally authored by Alan McLean at the New York Times, the DocumentViewer was subsequently open sourced, and taken over by Jeremy Ashkenas at DocumentCloud. Since DocumentCloud's migration to Investigative Reporters & Editors, the project has been maintained by Ted Han.

DocumentCloud is a two time Knight News Challenge winner, in 2009 and 2011. It was founded by Eric Umanski, Scott Klein and Aron Pilhofer, who are, respectively, Managing Editor at ProPublica, News Apps Editor at ProPublica, and Managing Editor at the New York Times. DocumentCloud's 2009-2011 team was Jeremy Ashkenas, Sam Clay, and Amanda Hickman. DocumentCloud's current team is Ted Han, Lauren Grandstaff and Nathan Stitt.
